## JobPrimaryCoverFront class

描述前（开始）封面。整个作业将只有一张主纸。 封面应打印在[`PageMediaSize`](../pagemediasize)和[`PageMediaType`](../pagemediatype) 用于作业的第一页。封面应该集成到加工选项 中（如[`JobDuplexAllDocumentsContiguously`](../jobduplexalldocumentscontiguously),[`JobNUpAllDocumentsContiguously`](../jobnupalldocumentscontiguously)) 由指定的选项指示。 https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/win32/printdocs/jobprimarycoverfront
