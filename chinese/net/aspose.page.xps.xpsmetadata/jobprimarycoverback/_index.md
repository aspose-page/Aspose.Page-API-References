## JobPrimaryCoverBack class

描述背面（结尾）封面。每个作业都有单独的主页。 封面页应打印在[`PageMediaSize`](../pagemediasize/)和[`PageMediaType`](../pagemediatype/) 用于作业的最后一页。封面应集成到处理选项 （例如[`JobDuplexAllDocumentsContiguously`](../jobduplexalldocumentscontiguously/),[`JobNUpAllDocumentsContiguously`](../jobnupalldocumentscontiguously/)) 由指定的选项指示。 https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/win32/printdocs/jobprimarycoverback
