## String::IsNormalized method

| Parámetro | Tipo | Descripción |
| --- | --- | --- |
| form | System::Text::NormalizationForm | Forma de normalización, vea [System::Text::NormalizationForm](../../../system.text/normalizationform/) para más detalles. |
