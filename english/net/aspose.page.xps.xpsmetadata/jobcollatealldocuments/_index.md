## JobCollateAllDocuments class

Describes the collating characteristics of the output. All documents in each individual job are collated. [`DocumentCollate`](../documentcollate/) and `JobCollateAllDocuments` are mutually exclusive. The behavior and implementation of whether both or only one of these keywords is implemented is left to the driver. https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/win32/printdocs/jobcollatealldocuments
