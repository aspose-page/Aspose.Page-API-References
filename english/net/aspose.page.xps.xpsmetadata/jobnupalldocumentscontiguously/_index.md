## JobNUpAllDocumentsContiguously class

Describes the output of multiple logical pages to a single physical sheet. All documents in the job are compiled together contiguously. `JobNUpAllDocumentsContiguously` and [`DocumentNUp`](../documentnup/) are mutually exclusive. It is up to the driver to determine constraint handling between these keywords. https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/win32/printdocs/jobnupalldocumentscontiguously
