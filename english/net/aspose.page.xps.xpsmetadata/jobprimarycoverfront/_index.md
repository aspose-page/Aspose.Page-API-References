## JobPrimaryCoverFront class

Describes the front (beginning) cover sheet. The entire job will have a single primary sheet. The cover sheet should be printed on the [`PageMediaSize`](../pagemediasize/) and [`PageMediaType`](../pagemediatype/) used for the first page of the job. The cover sheet should be integrated into processing options (such as [`JobDuplexAllDocumentsContiguously`](../jobduplexalldocumentscontiguously/), [`JobNUpAllDocumentsContiguously`](../jobnupalldocumentscontiguously/)) as indicated by the Option specified. https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/win32/printdocs/jobprimarycoverfront
