## JobStapleAllDocuments class

Describes the stapling characteristics of the output. All documents in the job are stapled together. The `JobStapleAllDocuments` and [`DocumentStaple`](../documentstaple/) keywords are mutually exclusive. It is up to the driver to determine constraint handling between these keywords. https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/win32/printdocs/jobstaplealldocuments
