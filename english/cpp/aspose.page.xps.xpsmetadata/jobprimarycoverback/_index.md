## JobPrimaryCoverBack class


Describes the back (ending) cover sheet. Each job will have a separate primary sheet. The cover sheet should be printed on the [PageMediaSize](../pagemediasize/) and [PageMediaType](../pagemediatype/) used for the final page of the job. The cover sheet should be integrated into processing options (such as [JobDuplexAllDocumentsContiguously](../jobduplexalldocumentscontiguously/), [JobNUpAllDocumentsContiguously](../jobnupalldocumentscontiguously/)) as indicated by the [Option](../option/) specified. [https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/win32/printdocs/jobprimarycoverback](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/win32/printdocs/jobprimarycoverback).
