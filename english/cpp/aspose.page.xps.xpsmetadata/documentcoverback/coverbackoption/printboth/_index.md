## PrintBoth field


Specifies the cover indicated by "CoverBackSource" may be printed on either sides of the cover sheet. If a [DocumentCoverBackSource](../../../documentcoverbacksource/)[ParameterInit](../../../parameterinit/) element is not specified, this [Option](../../../option/) should be ignored.
