## JobStapleAllDocuments class

Describes the stapling characteristics of the output. All documents in the job are stapled together.
The [`JobStapleAllDocuments`](/page/python-net/aspose.page.xps.xpsmetadata/jobstaplealldocuments) and [`DocumentStaple`](/page/python-net/aspose.page.xps.xpsmetadata/documentstaple) keywords are mutually exclusive.
It is up to the driver to determine constraint handling between these keywords.
