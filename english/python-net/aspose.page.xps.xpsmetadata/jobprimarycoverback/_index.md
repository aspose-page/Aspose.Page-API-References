## JobPrimaryCoverBack class

Describes the back (ending) cover sheet. Each job will have a separate primary sheet.
The cover sheet should be printed on the [`PageMediaSize`](/page/python-net/aspose.page.xps.xpsmetadata/pagemediasize) and [`PageMediaType`](/page/python-net/aspose.page.xps.xpsmetadata/pagemediatype)
used for the final page of the job. The cover sheet should be integrated into processing options
(such as [`JobDuplexAllDocumentsContiguously`](/page/python-net/aspose.page.xps.xpsmetadata/jobduplexalldocumentscontiguously), [`JobNUpAllDocumentsContiguously`](/page/python-net/aspose.page.xps.xpsmetadata/jobnupalldocumentscontiguously))
as indicated by the Option specified.
https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/win32/printdocs/jobprimarycoverback
