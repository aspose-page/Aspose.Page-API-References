## XmlWriterSettings::get_CloseOutput method


Mengembalikan nilai yang menunjukkan apakah [XmlWriter](../../xmlwriter/) juga harus menutup aliran atau TextWriter yang mendasarinya ketika metode [XmlWriter::Close](../../xmlwriter/close/) dipanggil.

```cpp
bool System::Xml::XmlWriterSettings::get_CloseOutput()
```


### ReturnValue

**true** to also close the underlying stream or TextWriter; otherwise, **false**. The default is **false**.
